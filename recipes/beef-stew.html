A delicious freshly Beef Stew

Beef Stew is a firm family favourite in any household.

Check out how to make it here!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Stock Cube
  • Stewing Veg Pack
  • Suet

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Make Stew
  3. Place into oven and cook for 5 hours on low
