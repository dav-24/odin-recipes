Lasagna

A delicious freshly cooked lasagna

Lasagna is a firm family favourite in any household.

Check out how to make it here!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • White sauce
  • Cheese

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Layer lasagna in a pasta dish
  3. Place into oven and cook for 45 minutes
  4. Remove from oven, sprinkle with cheese and return for another 15 mins before serving
